# Debt

Log and track outstanding debts owed by customers.

---

## Debt list

Go to **Operations → Debt**. The table shows each debt record's status, assigned team member, linked account, and amount outstanding.

### Filtering

Use the filter bar to narrow by status or assignee.

---

## Logging a debt

Click **New debt** and fill in the modal:

- **Amount** — the outstanding balance
- **Description** — context around the debt (e.g. missed payments, disputed invoice)
- **Assigned to** — the team member chasing it
- **Linked provisioning** *(optional)* — associates the customer account

---

## Debt detail

Click any row to open the record. From here you can:

- Update the amount and description
- Log payment attempts and record their outcomes
- Reassign to another team member
- Mark as resolved once the debt is cleared or written off
