# Deal Sheet

The form used to submit a new sale. It captures customer details, services, pricing, and billing in one place.

---

## Submitting a deal

Go to **Sales → Deal Sheet** and work through each section:

### 1. Customer details

Enter the customer's title, first name, last name, and contact number. For business accounts, add the company name.

### 2. Sales agents

Select the primary sales agent. Add a secondary agent if a second person is involved.

Then choose a call tariff:

- Unlimited Calls to UK Landlines
- Unlimited Calls to UK Landlines & Mobiles
- Standard Call Package — 2p/min landlines, 10p/min mobiles

For international calls, select an add-on or **N/A**.

### 5. Equipment

Select handsets and quantities. Available options include mobile app, Yealink handsets, desktop handsets, and cordless bundles.

### 6. Pricing

Enter the monthly bundle price charged to the customer. Wholesale cost and gross profit (GP) are calculated automatically. Enter the contract length in months.

### 7. Billing

Enter the customer's billing address and direct debit details.

---

## After submission

A deal record is created in **Sales → Deals** with a status of **Open**. The provisioning team picks it up from there. See the [Deals guide](/help/deals) for how to track it.
