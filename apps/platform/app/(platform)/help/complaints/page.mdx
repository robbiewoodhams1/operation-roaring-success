# Complaints

Log, track, and resolve customer complaints.

---

## Complaints list

Go to **Operations → Complaints**. The table shows each complaint's status, assigned team member, linked account, and when it was opened.

### Filtering

Use the filter bar to narrow by status or assignee.

---

## Creating a complaint

Click **New complaint** and fill in the modal:

- **Description** — what the complaint is about
- **Assigned to** — the team member handling it
- **Linked provisioning** *(optional)* — attaches the customer account automatically

---

## Complaint detail

Click any row to open the record. From here you can:

- Update the description and current status
- Reassign to a different team member
- Change the linked provisioning order
- Set a resolution date and mark as resolved
