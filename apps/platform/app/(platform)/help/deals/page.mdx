# Deals

Track all sales deals — from submission through to completion.

---

## Deals list

Go to **Sales → Deals**. The table shows:

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Column</th>
      <th>Description</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Date</strong></td>
      <td>When the deal was submitted</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Status</strong></td>
      <td>Open, Close, or Closed</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Type</strong></td>
      <td>Deal type (e.g. new connection, upgrade)</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Agent(s)</strong></td>
      <td>Sales agents assigned to the deal</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Customer</strong></td>
      <td>The customer this deal is for</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Bundle price</strong></td>
      <td>Monthly price charged to the customer</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>GP</strong></td>
      <td>Gross profit — bundle price minus wholesale cost</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Contract</strong></td>
      <td>Contract length in months</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

### Filtering

Use the filter bar to narrow by status, agent, or date range.

---

## Deal detail

Click any row to open the record. From here you can:

- Update the deal status
- Edit services — broadband type, speeds, voice options, and equipment
- Edit pricing — bundle price, wholesale costs, GP, and contract length
- Edit billing information
- Navigate to the linked customer record
