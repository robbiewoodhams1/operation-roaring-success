# Transfers & Ceases

Track requests to transfer services to another provider or to cease (cancel) services entirely.

---

## List view

Go to **Operations → Transfers &amp; Ceases**. The table shows:

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Column</th>
      <th>Description</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Type</strong></td>
      <td>Transfer or Cease</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Status</strong></td>
      <td>Current stage of the request</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Account</strong></td>
      <td>Linked provisioning order and customer name</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Assigned to</strong></td>
      <td>Team member handling the request</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Opened</strong></td>
      <td>Date the request was raised</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Completed</strong></td>
      <td>Date it was finished, if applicable</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

### Filtering

Use the filter bar to narrow by type, status, or assignee.

---

## Creating a record

Click **New** and fill in the modal:

- **Type** — Transfer or Cease
- **Linked provisioning** — the order this request relates to
- **Assigned to** — who is handling it
- **Notes** — any context or customer instructions

---

## Record detail

Click any row to open the detail page. From here you can update the status, add notes, change the assignee, and record a completion date once processed.
