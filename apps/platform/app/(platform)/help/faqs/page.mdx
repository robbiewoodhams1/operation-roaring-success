# FAQs

Answers to common questions about the platform.

---

## Access & accounts

**I can't log in — what should I do?**

Check you are using the correct email address. If your account status is "pending", it has not been approved yet — contact your administrator. Use the **Forgot password** link on the login page if you have lost your password.

**My account says "pending" — what does that mean?**

New accounts require approval before you can access the platform. An administrator will approve your account, at which point your status changes to "active".

**I can't see a section in the sidebar.**

Some sections are role-restricted. Users and Audit Logs are only visible to Admins. If you believe you should have access, ask your administrator to check your role.

## Customers

**How do I find a customer?**

Use **Tools → Search** and type the customer name or account number. You can also browse via **Operations → Customers** and filter the list.

**Can I change a customer's status?**

Yes — open the customer record, update the status field, and save.

## Deals

**How do I submit a new deal?**

Go to **Sales → Deal Sheet**. See the [Deal Sheet guide](/help/deal-sheet) for a full walkthrough.

**What is GP?**

Gross Profit — the difference between the bundle price charged to the customer and the wholesale cost to the business.

**A deal is showing the wrong status.**

Open the deal from **Sales → Deals** and update the status field directly.

## Provisioning

**How do I log a provisioning attempt?**

Open the provisioning record and go to the relevant service section (BB, WHC, NFON, or MPF). Use the attempt controls to record the outcome.

**What do the service codes mean?**

See the [Provisioning guide](/help/provisioning) for a full explanation of BB, WHC, NFON, and MPF.

---

## Tasks

**What's the difference between the Home to-do panel and the full To Do page?**

The Home panel is for quick personal tasks stored only in your browser — they are private. The [To Do](/help/todo) page saves tasks to the database, supports priorities, links to records, and lets you assign tasks to teammates.
