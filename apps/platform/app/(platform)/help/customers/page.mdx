# Customers

View, search, and manage all customer accounts for your organisation.

---

## Customer list

Go to **Operations → Customers**. The table shows each customer's account number, name, type, and status.

### Filtering

Use the filter bar above the table to narrow by status. Use the search box to find customers by name or account number.

---

## Customer detail

Click any row to open the detail page. From here you can:

- View and edit all contact and address information
- See linked provisioning orders and deals for this customer
- Update the customer's status
