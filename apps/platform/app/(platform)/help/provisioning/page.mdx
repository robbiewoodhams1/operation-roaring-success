# Provisioning

Track broadband and telephony orders from placement through to go-live.

---

### Filtering

Use the filter bar to narrow by status, provisioner, or date range.

---

## Provisioning detail

Click any row to open the full record. From here you can:

- Update order dates and overall status
- Edit service-specific details — broadband type, speeds, ONT equipment, voice options
- View the linked customer and deal
- Log provisioning attempts and review attempt history per service
- Use the **Provision** button to trigger provisioning actions
