# Home

Your personal dashboard. Shows live metrics, a quick task list, and a feed of your recent platform activity.

---

### Managing your stat cards

- **Add a stat** — click the dashed **Add stat** card and pick from the list.
- **Remove a stat** — hover over any card and click the × in the top-right corner.

---

## To do

The To Do panel lets you add quick personal tasks without leaving the home page.

- Type a task and press **Enter** or click **+** to add it.
- Click the checkbox to mark it done — completed tasks collapse below the list.
- Hover and click × to delete a task.

> Tasks added here are stored locally in your browser and are private to you. For tasks you want to assign to teammates or link to records, use the full [To Do](/help/todo) page.

---

## Recent activity

Below the stats, the activity feed shows your own recent changes — records you've created, updated, or deleted. Each entry shows the action type, a short description, and the time it occurred.
