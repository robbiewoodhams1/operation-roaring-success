## Getting started

The platform is organised into several sections accessible from the sidebar:

**Operations** — Your day-to-day tools. Customers, provisioning, routers and faults all live here.

**Sales** — The deal sheet for submitting new deals and the deals list for tracking existing ones.

**Tools** — Search across the entire platform, view stats, and track targets.

If you have questions not covered here, speak to your administrator.
