# Targets

Track performance against goals across Sales, Provisioning, Debt, Tech, and ETF. Access via **Tools → Targets** in the sidebar.

---

## Sales targets

Shows deal performance by agent for the current month.

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Metric</th>
      <th>Description</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Deals submitted</strong></td>
      <td>Total deals logged this month</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Closes achieved</strong></td>
      <td>Deals moved to Closed status</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>MTD GP</strong></td>
      <td>Gross profit generated month-to-date</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

Each agent's row breaks down performance by deal type.

---

## Provisioning targets

Tracks service provisioning performance by type (BB, WHC, NFON, MPF). Shows both team-level and individual breakdowns.

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Metric</th>
      <th>Description</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Attempt count</strong></td>
      <td>Number of provisioning attempts per service</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Success rate</strong></td>
      <td>Percentage of successful attempts</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Live orders</strong></td>
      <td>Orders currently live vs target</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Debt targets

Tracks progress against debt collection goals — the volume of debt being resolved relative to monthly targets.

---

## Tech targets

Tracks technical KPIs relevant to the tech team's performance goals.

## ETF targets

Tracks Early Termination Fee collection metrics and targets.
