# To Do

A shared task manager for tracking work across the team. Create tasks for yourself, assign them to teammates, and link them to records in the platform.

---

## Viewing tasks

Go to **Tools → To Do**. The page has two tabs:

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Tab</th>
      <th>Shows</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>My tasks</strong></td>
      <td>Tasks assigned to you — by yourself or by a teammate</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Assigned by me</strong></td>
      <td>Tasks you have assigned to other people</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

Pending tasks appear at the top. Completed tasks are collapsed below and can be expanded.

---

## Creating a task

Click **New task** and fill in the modal:

- **Title** — short description of what needs doing *(required)*
- **Description** — additional context *(optional)*
- **Priority** — Low, Medium, or High
- **Assigned to** — yourself or a teammate
- **Link** *(optional)* — attach the task to a specific record: Customer, Provisioning order, Fault, Complaint, or Debt

Linked records are displayed on the task so you can navigate directly to them.

---

## Managing tasks

- **Complete** — click the checkbox on the left. The task moves to the completed section.
- **Delete** — hover and click the trash icon on the right.

---

## Quick tasks on the Home page

The [Home](/help/home) dashboard has a lightweight To Do panel for quick personal tasks. These are stored in your browser only and are not shared with teammates. Use this page for anything you need to assign or track properly.
