# Routers

Submit and record router provisioning and replacement requests for customer equipment.

---

## Using the form

Go to **Operations → Routers**. Fill in the relevant fields and submit the form. The information captured is used by the team to action the request.

---

## Tips

- If you are unsure which router model applies, check the customer's provisioning record — FTTP and SOGEA lines may require different equipment.
- If a router needs replacing due to a reported fault, log a [Fault](/help/faults) record first and reference it when completing the form.
