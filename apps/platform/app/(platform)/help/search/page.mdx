# Search

Find any record across the platform from a single search box.

---

## Using search

Go to **Tools → Search** and type into the search input. Results are returned across three areas simultaneously:

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Area</th>
      <th>Matched by</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Customers</strong></td>
      <td>Name or account number</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Deals</strong></td>
      <td>Customer name, deal reference, or agent</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><strong>Provisioning</strong></td>
      <td>Account number or customer name</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

Click any result to navigate directly to that record.

---

## Tips

- You do not need to know where a record lives — search covers all three areas at once.
- An account number gives the most precise result.
- Search is scoped to your organisation — you will only see records belonging to your tenant.
