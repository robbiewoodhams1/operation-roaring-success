# Account

View your profile information and sign out of the platform.

---

## Your profile

Access your account via the link at the bottom of the sidebar. The page shows your email, full name, role, approval status, and the organisation you belong to.

## Signing out

Click **Sign out** on the account page to end your session. You will be returned to the login page.

---

## Changing your details

Contact your administrator to update your name, email, or role. Admins can manage accounts via **Admin → Users**.
