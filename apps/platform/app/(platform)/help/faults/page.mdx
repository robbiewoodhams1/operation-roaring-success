# Faults

Log and track service faults affecting customers.

---

## Faults list

Go to **Operations → Faults**. The table shows each fault's status, assigned team member, linked account, and when it was reported.

### Filtering

Use the filter bar to narrow by status or assignee.

---

## Logging a fault

Click **New fault** and fill in the modal:

- **Description** — what the fault is and how it was reported
- **Assigned to** — the team member investigating it
- **Linked provisioning** *(optional)* — attaches the customer account automatically

---

## Fault detail

Click any row to open the record. From here you can:

- Update the description and investigation notes
- Reassign to a different team member
- Progress the status as the investigation moves forward
- Set a resolution date once the fault is fixed
